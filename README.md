# -Cost-Sensitive-Predictive-Modeling
The objective is to develop a high-performance, cost-effective predictive model to 
identify customers most likely to convert on a marketing offer. It is based on anonymised data of 5000 customers in training set and 5000 customers in test set, but the test labels
are not provided, as they will be the evaluation basis. The primary challenge is balancing revenue maximization against the costs of data acquisition and the negative impact of "Marketing Fatigue".The model acts as a precise filter to identify individuals who provide the highest net value to the firm while treating customer attention as a finite resource.
